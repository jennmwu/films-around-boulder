Nothing beats catching a film on the big screen, whether it's opening night with a packed house, a 35mm print passing through town, or a re-release of a classic. I got tired of missing out just because I forgot to check all the different theater websites around town. So I built Films Around Boulder to keep track of what's playing across Boulder and the Front Range, for me, my friends, and you too!
